About Dazzle and Delight

Dazzle and Delight is your premier event management platform, designed to enhance the way you experience events in your locality. From concerts to community gatherings, we provide an easy-to-use interface for both organizers and attendees.

Our Features

  • User Roles: Tailored experiences for administrators, event organizers, and customers.
  • Event Browsing: Find events by location and date.
  • Online Booking: Seamlessly book your tickets online.
  • Feedback System: Share your experiences and help us improve.

Technologies Used

Our platform is developed using modern technologies to ensure a smooth experience:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Framework: Bootstrap
