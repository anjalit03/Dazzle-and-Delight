Frequently Asked
Questions

Is it free to use Dazzle and Delight?

Browsing and account creation are free - you only pay for event tickets you purchase.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Refund policies vary by event - full refund 7+ days before, partial refund 3-7 days before, no refund within 72 hours.

How do I receive my tickets?

Tickets are emailed to you and available in your account and mobile app immediately after purchase.

Do you offer international booking services?

Yes, we accept orders from over 50 countries. International tariffs and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.

How does your loyalty program work?

Our loyalty program rewards you with points for every purchase. You earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases. You also get exclusive access to members-only sales and early access to new collections.

Are there any ongoing sales or promotions?

Check our "Sales" section for current discounts. We also run seasonal promotions and flash sales. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals.
